EDUCATION

I currently call St. Joseph's University home and I absolutely love it here. I attended Mercy High School in Farmington Hills, MI until graduating in 2011. I will graduate from St. Joe's in the spring of 2016 after completing my fifth year.

When I came on my tour at St. Joe's I realized almost immediately that it was the right fit for me. Once I learned about the Food Marketing program, I absolutely knew it was a match made in heaven. Food has always been my passion, whether it means eating it or cooking it, and I knew that I couldn't find a major that was more appropriate.

At the end of my sophomore year of college, I realized that I wanted an extra challenge in my studies. After investigating various majors and minors I discovered communications and decided it was a good fit with my Food Marketing degree. That spring, I declared my second major and there was no turning back.

In the summer between my sophomore and junior year I was fortunate enough to study abroad in the world's most beautiful place, Greece. I am absolutely dying to return someday!

I am truly blessed each and every day to call this amazing university my home. I have been offered countless opportunities that I would have never been able to experience anywhere else and met so many incredible people who have changed my life for the better. I am sure that when May 2016 rolls around and it is my turn to walk across that stage, diploma in hand, I will be one sad girl but as for right now I am just going to savor every minute of it. Go Hawks!
