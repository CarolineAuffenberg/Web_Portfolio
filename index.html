WELCOME

A 21 year old native of Michigan who is currently living in the City of Brotherly Love and enjoying every minute of it. I am a senior Food Marketing and Communications double major at Saint Joseph's University. Someday I hope to open my very own restaurant but for now you can catch me in the hustle and bustle of college life.

I think my biggest passion in life without a doubt is food. Whether it means cooking or eating, I absolutely love it. My favorite foods include crab cakes, mexican cuisine, cheese, bacon, peanut butter, and of course anything with chocolate! Aside from that, I am an avid sports fan (especially Alabama football) and a bookworm. I especially love the summertime and every activity that involves the water. I grew up learning how to swim and sail at home in Michigan.

I suffer from severe wanderlust but hopefully in the future I will be able to travel and eat my way through a multitude of different places.
